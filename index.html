What are Under and Over 0.5 Goals?

Under and Over 0.5 Goals is a football betting market where bettors predict whether a match will have zero goals (under 0.5) or at least one goal (over 0.5).

Over 0.5 goals means at least one goal will be scored in the match. Under 0.5 goals means no goals will be scored.

This market is popular because most football matches in leagues like the Premier League or Serie A average more than 0.5 goals per game.
